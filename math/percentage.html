Meaning of Percent

Percent is out of 100. We need to conver the fraction, decimal or number to a fraction with 100 in the denominator ot get a "out-of-100" value.

Convert fractions to percentage

  1. Look at the fraction
  2. Does it have a multiple of 100 in the denominator?
  3. Multiple of 100 means a number with two or more zeros at the end
  4. If the denominator is not a multiple of 100 then we need to convert it
  5. Multiply the numerator and denominator by 100 to do so

Question - what denominators are a multiple of 100?

$frac{3}{4}$ , $frac{13}{400}$ , $frac{23}{10}$ , $frac{123}{4780}$, $frac{100}{15}$

$frac{3}{4}$ has 4 as denominator, not a multiple of 100

$frac{13}{400}$ has two zeros, a multiple of 100, since 400 = 4 $times$ 100

$frac{23}{10}$ has only one zero at the end, we need two zeros

$frac{100}{15}$ note that we need a multiple of 100 in denominator

$frac{123}{4780}$ has only one zero, we need two zeros

Question - How do we convert the denominators?

$frac{3}{4} = frac{3}{4} times frac{100}{100} = frac{300}{400} = frac{300}{4} times frac{1}{100}$
↑ we need two zeros, so we multiply by 100.

$frac{13}{400} = frac{13}{4} times frac{1}{100} $
↑ already has two zeros at the end. No need to do anything.

$frac{23}{10} = frac{23}{10} times frac{10}{10} = frac{230}{100} = frac{230}{1} times frac{1}{100}$
↑ one zero at the end and we need one more.

$frac{100}{15} = frac{100}{15} times frac{100}{100} = frac{10000}{1500} = frac{10000}{15} times frac{1}{100}$
↑ we need two zeros, so multiply and divide by 100.

What if we have more than two zeros in the denominator?

$frac{25}{4000} = frac{25}{40} times frac{1}{100} $
↑ we have three zeros, we need two so leave one with the number.

$frac{625}{10000} = frac{625}{100} times frac{1}{100} $
↑ we have four zeros, we need two so leave two with the number.

Calculate percentages

$frac{1}{100}$ is "1-out-of-100" so $frac{1}{100}$ is just the percentage (%) sign.

Hence $frac{3}{4} = frac{300}{4} times frac{1}{100} = frac{300}{4} % = 75% $

$frac{23}{10} = frac{230}{1} times frac{1}{100} = 230 %$

$5 = frac{5}{1} times frac{100}{100} = frac{500}{1} times frac{1}{100} = 500 %$

$frac{625}{10000} = frac{625}{100} times frac{1}{100} = frac{625}{100} % = 6.25% $

Decimals to percentages

$ 0.007 = frac{7}{1000}$
The denominator has more than two zeros. We leave one zero with the number.
$ frac{7}{1000} = frac{7}{10} times frac{1}{100} = frac{7}{10} % = 0.7 %$

$ 0.025 = frac{25}{1000}$
The denominator has more than two zeros. We leave one zero with the number.
$ frac{25}{1000} = frac{25}{10} times frac{1}{100} = frac{25}{10} % = 2.5 %$

$ 2.5 = frac{25}{10}$
The denominator has one zero. We need one more.
$ frac{25}{10} = frac{25}{10} times frac{10}{10} = frac{250}{100} = frac{250}{1} times frac{1}{100} = 250%$

Convert Percentages

Remember that the percentage sign stands for 1-out-of-100 or $frac{1}{100}$.
The percentage sign can simply be replaced by $times frac{1}{100}$.

$ 5 % = 5 times frac{1}{100} = frac{5}{100} = 0.05 $
$ 35 % = 35 times frac{1}{100} = frac{35}{100} = 0.35 $
$ 12frac{1}{2} % = frac{25}{2} % = frac{25}{2} times frac{1}{100} = frac{25}{200} = frac{1}{8}$

Simplification of fractions

The fractions should be converted to their simplest forms. To do so, write the expanded form of numerator and denominator and cancel the common numbers.

$ frac{25}{200} = frac{ 5 times 5 } {2 times 2 times 2 times 5 times 5} = frac{ cancel(5) times cancel(5) } {2 times 2 times 2 times cancel(5) times cancel(5)} = frac{1} {8} $

Examples

$ 15 % = 15 times frac{1}{100} = frac{15}{100} = frac{3 times cancel(5)}{2 times 2 times 5 times cancel(5)} = frac{3}{20}$
$ 45 % = 45 times frac{1}{100} = frac{45}{100} = frac{3 times 3 times cancel(5)}{2 times 2 times 5 times cancel(5)} = frac{9}{20}$
$ 0.35 % = 0.35 times frac{1}{100} = frac{35}{100} times frac{1}{100} = frac{35}{10000} $
$ rArr frac{35}{10000} = frac{5 times 7}{100 times 100} = frac{cancel(5) times 7}{cancel(100) 20 times 100} = frac{7}{2000}$
$ rArr frac{7}{2000} = frac{7 times 1 }{2 times 1000} = frac{7}{2} times frac{1}{1000} = frac{3.5}{1000} = 0.035$
